Chapter 1: The Setup - Making the Law Work for You

Let's rewind to July 18, 2025. President Trump signed the GENIUS Act into law, creating America's first federal regulatory framework for stablecoins. The media covered it as historic crypto legislation.

What they missed was the setup.

The Legislative Insider Trade

While Congress debated the GENIUS Act, the Trump family was quietly building World Liberty Financial, their "decentralized finance platform." The timing wasn't coincidental:

🏛️ March-June 2025: Trump publicly advocates for the GENIUS Act, calling it essential for American crypto leadership

💰 June 2025: World Liberty Financial finalizes its stablecoin architecture

⚖️ July 18: Trump signs GENIUS Act into law

🚀 July 20: World Liberty launches USD1 stablecoin, instantly becoming one of America's largest stablecoin issuers

The Conflict: Trump wasn't just signing crypto legislation—he was signing legislation that would directly enrich his family's crypto business. As one ethics expert told NPR, Trump became "both the regulator and the regulated" in the same industry.

This wasn't regulatory capture. This was regulatory authorship.
