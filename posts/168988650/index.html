Presidential Weekend Dreams from the Oval Office

Setting: Oval Office, dreaming of this weekend's escape to Mar-a-Lago

July 22, 2025

"Finally. FINALLY. I will be out of that swamp this weekend. You know what's beautiful? Air Force One. Tremendous plane. The most incredible plane ever built - I basically redesigned the whole thing, by the way. Nobody talks about that.

What a week. What an absolutely rigged, unfair, totally biased week. The fake news media was out of control - more than usual, which is saying something. And Congress! Don't get me started on Congress. Bunch of lightweights asking me questions like they don't know I'm the greatest dealmaker in history.

Can't wait to head home. Mar-a-Lago - the most beautiful property you've ever seen. Probably the most beautiful property in the world, actually. People tell me all the time, 'Sir, we've never seen anything like Mar-a-Lago.' And they're right.

I'm going to play golf this weekend... why not, it's on the taxpayer's dime. Golf will fix everything. Tiger will probably call me - he always does - and he'll tell me my swing is looking tremendous. 'Most natural golfer I've ever seen,' he'll say. The media won't report that, of course.

I'm going to have the most incredible dinner on the terrace Saturday night. The terrace at Mar-a-Lago is incredible. World leaders will cry - literally cry - when they see that terrace again. 'How did you create something so beautiful?' they'll ask me.

Note to self: I'm going to fire someone when I get back Monday. Always fire someone after a rough week. It'll keep everyone sharp."

[Daydream interrupted by another briefing about boring policy stuff]
