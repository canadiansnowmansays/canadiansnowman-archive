Last night, Ilhan Omar was physically attacked at a Minneapolis town hall.

She became a Congresswoman. She gained a national platform. She refused to be intimidated. She kept speaking.

For Trump, this is intolerable.

Trump’s Seven-Year Obsession

This didn’t start with the financial investigation. Trump has been attacking Ilhan Omar since 2019.

At a Trump rally in North Carolina, the crowd chanted “SEND HER BACK!” about Omar (and other Squad members).

Trump let the chant continue for 13 seconds before starting to speak again. He later claimed he “disagreed” with the chant, but he did nothing to stop it at the time.

The chant was explicitly racist: Omar is a naturalized U.S. citizen. She has as much right to be here as anyone. The implication—that she should be “sent back” to Somalia—treats citizenship as conditional based on race and origin.

September 2019: “She’s Lucky to Be Where She Is”

Trump tweeted: “Ilhan Omar is an ungrateful wretch. She’s lucky to be where she is, lucky to be in this country.”

Translation: You should be grateful we let you in. Don’t criticize us.

This is the language of conditional belonging. You can be here, but only if you’re silent.

The Financial “Crime”: The Letitia James Playbook Redux

Let’s examine what Omar is actually accused of.

The claim (spreading across right-wing media): Ilhan Omar’s net worth jumped from negative $65,000 in 2018 to $30-44 million in 2024. This is impossible on a congressional salary. Therefore: fraud.

The reality (per Snopes fact-check and financial disclosure analysis):

  1. She got married in 2020 to Timothy Mynett, a political consultant and entrepreneur who owns several businesses.

  2. Financial disclosure forms require members of Congress to report HOUSEHOLD assets, not just personal assets.

  3. Mynett’s businesses are valued at estimated ranges on the disclosure forms (as is standard—filers give ranges, not exact figures).

  4. The businesses are valued between $1 million and $5 million each on the disclosure forms.

  5. Her HOUSEHOLD net worth (including spouse’s assets) is estimated at $6-30 million.

  6. Her INDIVIDUAL net worth remains negative $82,000 to positive $65,000.

In other words: When you marry someone who owns businesses, and you have to report household finances, your household net worth includes their businesses.

This is exactly what happened with Jesse Watters’ own wife—when she married him, her household net worth increased by virtue of becoming Mrs. Jesse Watters. But Fox News isn’t investigating that.

This is the Letitia James playbook:

Remember James? She paid off her mortgage early and saved $50/month. They called it fraud. A grand jury indicted her. Then Judge Currie dismissed it. Then two more grand juries in two different cities refused to re-indict her.

The pattern:

  1. Find a financial transaction

  2. Strip all context

  3. Make it sound suspicious

  4. Demand investigation

  5. Launch prosecution

  6. Hope something sticks

With James, it was a mortgage. With Omar, it’s marriage.

Both cases have one thing in common: Women of color who prosecuted or criticized Trump.

The Minnesota Context: ICE and the Somali Community

The timing of this investigation isn’t coincidental. It’s happening amid a massive ICE crackdown in Minnesota.

January 25, 2026 (yesterday): ICE agents fatally shot Alex Pretti, a 37-year-old nurse, during Operation “Metro Surge” in Minneapolis.

This came after ICE killed Renee Good earlier this month, also in Minneapolis.

Minnesota has the largest Somali community in the United States, concentrated in Minneapolis—Omar’s district.

Operation “Metro Surge” is Trump’s targeted ICE operation in Minnesota, aimed at this community.

Trump announced today that he’s sending Tom Homan (his border czar) to Minnesota. Trump also claimed there’s “$20 billion welfare fraud” in Minnesota, targeting the Somali community.

The message is clear: We’re coming for your community. We’re coming for your representatives. We’re making you all look like criminals.

Omar represents Minnesota’s 5th District, which includes Minneapolis. She’s been vocally opposing the ICE operations and the killings.

So Trump:

  • Sends ICE to crack down on Somali community

  • Announces investigation into largest welfare fraud

  • Demands DOJ investigate the Somali-American Congresswoman

  • Calls Somalis “very low IQ people” and “garbage”

This is collective punishment. This is using law enforcement to terrorize a community for its political opposition.
