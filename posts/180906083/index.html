A Sunday Satire on Modern Feudalism and the Art of the Bow

MEMORANDUM: Required Protocols for Interactions with POTUS 45/47

THE SELF-CORONATION PROTOCOL

December 5, 2025 | Kennedy Center, Washington, D.C.

At precisely the moment FIFA President Gianni Infantino presented the inaugural FIFA Peace Prize to President Donald J. Trump, keen observers witnessed a masterclass in modern feudal protocol. The medal—gold, naturally—was offered. But rather than waiting for the tribute-bearer to perform the traditional placement around the neck, the President took the medal and placed it on himself.

The symbolism was perfect: When even your most devoted courtiers cannot crown you quickly enough, you crown yourself.

Infantino, demonstrating proper supplicant form, had already delivered the required incantations: “This is what we want from a leader—a leader that cares about the people... You definitely deserve the first FIFA Peace Prize for your action, for what you have obtained in your way, but you have obtained it in an incredible way. You can always count, Mr. President, on my support.”

Protocol Note: The prize was created in November 2024, mere weeks after President Trump failed to receive the Nobel Peace Prize, which instead went to Venezuelan opposition leader Maria Corina Machado. When existing institutions refuse to bestow appropriate honors, loyal courtiers must invent new ones. This represents advanced-level tribute protocol.

Timing Note: The award was presented less than 24 hours after Trump administration air strikes in the Caribbean. The juxtaposition between “peace prize” and military action is irrelevant to proper tribute protocol. What matters is the bow, not the body count.

The President’s response demonstrated appropriate self-regard: “This is truly one of the great honors of my life.” The emphasis on “truly” and “great” should be studied by all supplicants as examples of proper self-valorization.

Additional Context: Infantino has made multiple White House visits, attended Trump’s second inauguration, and joined the October ceremony in Egypt for the Gaza truce. This represents the minimum frequency of tribute required to maintain good standing. Infantino also received the honor of presenting the World Cup trophy in the Oval Office in August 2025, where he praised Trump for setting “new records on ticket sales.”

PART I: THE TRIBUTE REGISTRY

A Comprehensive Catalog of Offerings to the Throne

The following section documents required tribute patterns for maintaining access to power. Study these examples carefully. Your career, business interests, and possibly your freedom depend on mastering these techniques.

Category A: Corporate Feudal Oaths

The Bezos Capitulation (October 2024)

Initial Position: The Washington Post maintained editorial independence, had been critical of Trump’s first term.

Critical Background Context: In October 2018, Washington Post columnist Jamal Khashoggi entered the Saudi consulate in Istanbul. He never left alive. U.S. intelligence concluded that Saudi Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman personally ordered Khashoggi’s murder. The journalist was killed and dismembered—literally losing not just his head, but his entire body.

Trump’s response to the murder of The Washington Post’s own columnist? He defended MBS, emphasized Saudi arms purchases (”$110 billion. That’s a lot of jobs”), and accepted Saudi denials. “Maybe he did, maybe he didn’t,” Trump said of MBS’s involvement.

The Pivot: Six years after his newspaper’s columnist was murdered and dismembered by a regime Trump defended, Jeff Bezos:

  • Killed planned endorsement of Kamala Harris just days before 2024 election

  • Personally intervened to block editorial board’s decision

  • Held subsequent meetings with Trump

  • Blue Origin received favorable government contracts

Protocol Assessment: The irony is exquisite. The Washington Post’s motto—”Democracy Dies in Darkness”—remained on the masthead while its owner demonstrated that capitalism dies in daylight, exposed to presidential retaliation. Bezos understood that waiting until after the election would require greater tribute. By bowing early, he demonstrated both wisdom and fear—the two qualities most valued in courtiers.

The Lesson: When the President defends the regime that murdered and dismembered your newspaper’s journalist, the correct response is not righteous indignation. It is submission. Six years later, you kill your editorial board’s endorsement of his opponent and pay tribute at Mar-a-Lago.

Advanced Protocol Note: Bezos’s capitulation sent a clear message to every other media owner: Even if they murder your employees, if the President defends them, you bow to the President.

The Zuckerberg Conversion (Late 2024-2025)

Initial Position: Meta/Facebook maintained fact-checking programs, had banned President Trump following January 6, 2021.

The Pivot:

  • Announced termination of third-party fact-checking program (January 2025)

  • $1 million donation to Trump’s inauguration fund

  • Public statements praising Trump’s “leadership”

  • Personal dinner at Mar-a-Lago

Protocol Assessment: Excellent execution. The key was not merely reversing policy, but doing so with sufficient public genuflection to signal complete submission. Note that the monetary tribute accompanied the policy reversal—this is not coincidence. Gold alone is insufficient; public declarations of fealty must accompany the treasure.

Quote for Study: Zuckerberg’s public statements transformed from concerns about democracy to celebration of “free expression” under Trump’s leadership.

The Tech CEO Pilgrimage (December 2024-January 2025)

A parade of Silicon Valley leaders made the journey to Mar-a-Lago, each bearing gifts:

  • Tim Cook (Apple): Multiple visits, praised Trump’s “pro-business” agenda

  • Sundar Pichai (Google): Sought forgiveness for perceived slights during first term

  • Sam Altman (OpenAI): Pledged cooperation with administration

  • Elon Musk: Special status as court jester/consigliere (see separate protocols)

Protocol Assessment: The pilgrimage is mandatory. Each CEO understood that regulatory agencies, antitrust enforcement, and government contracts flow from the throne. Those who failed to make the journey (list remains classified) faced immediate consequences.

Category B: The Cabinet Praise Circle

Required Protocol: Cabinet meetings must begin with a round-robin of tributes to POTUS. Each member must:

  1. Thank the President for the “honor” of serving

  2. Cite specific achievements of the administration

  3. Use superlatives (”greatest,” “most successful,” “unprecedented”)

  4. Make eye contact while praising

  5. Never, under any circumstances, center oneself in the narrative

Historical Example - June 2017 Cabinet Meeting:

Reince Priebus: “We thank you for the opportunity and the blessing to serve your agenda.”

Mike Pence: “This is the greatest privilege of my life, to serve as vice president to a president who’s keeping his word to the American people.”

Jeff Sessions: “It is an honor to be able to serve you.”

Ryan Zinke: “I am privileged to be your steward of our public lands.”

Wilbur Ross: “I’m privileged to be here — I’m very excited about the commerce agenda.”

Protocol Assessment: This 2017 example set the standard for the second term. Note the repetition of “privilege,” “honor,” and “blessing.” These are not coincidental word choices. They signal proper understanding of the feudal relationship.

2025 Update: Cabinet meetings now begin with a formal pledge: “I serve at the pleasure of President Trump and pledge my complete loyalty to his agenda.” Those who hesitate during the pledge are noted.

Additional Protocol Note: During extended cabinet meetings, POTUS may enter a state of “strategic contemplation” (eyes closed, occasional head nodding). This is a sign of deep processing and should NOT be interpreted as sleep. Cabinet members must continue their reports with equal enthusiasm regardless of POTUS’s contemplation state. Those who pause their praise, lower their voice, or exchange glances during these contemplation periods are noted.

Critical Reminder: If POTUS appears to be in strategic contemplation when it is your turn to speak, you must:

  1. Continue speaking at normal volume

  2. Maintain enthusiasm in your delivery

  3. Never, under any circumstances, say “Mr. President?” to check alertness

  4. Assume he is absorbing every word even if he appears to be in deep REM-stage contemplation

Historical Note: During a July 2025 cabinet meeting, one agency head made the error of pausing mid-tribute when POTUS entered strategic contemplation. The pause lasted 4.5 seconds. The agency head was dismissed within the week for “lack of energy and enthusiasm.”

Category C: Foreign Tribute

The Infantino Innovation

As documented in the opening, FIFA President Gianni Infantino represents the gold standard (literally) of international tribute:

  • Created entirely new award when existing institutions proved inadequate

  • Made multiple White House appearances

  • Praised Trump for “ending eight wars” (specific wars remain unverified)

  • Presented tribute at internationally televised event

  • Pledged “the support of the entire football community” to Trump’s peace efforts

Context Note: FIFA, an organization synonymous with corruption for decades, awarded a “peace prize” to a president who, days earlier, had called Somali immigrants “garbage” and authorized Caribbean military strikes. The disconnect between rhetoric and reality is irrelevant to proper tribute protocol.

Quote for Study: “This is what we want from a leader: a leader that cares about the people. We want to live in a safe world, in a safe environment. We want to unite, and that’s what we do here today.”

Translation: “I want to maintain my position as FIFA president and ensure the 2026 World Cup proceeds smoothly in the United States. I will say whatever is necessary.”

The Netanyahu Alignment

Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu demonstrates sustained tribute protocols:

  • Named Golan Heights settlement after Trump

  • Praised Trump’s “historic” Middle East policies

  • Coordinated all major policy announcements with White House

  • Provided Trump with regular briefings presented as intelligence sharing

  • Ensured Trump received credit for Abraham Accords

Protocol Assessment: Netanyahu understands that American military aid and diplomatic cover require constant reinforcement through public praise. The relationship is transactional but dressed in the language of friendship. This is proper protocol.

Category D: The Republican Congressional Bow

Required Protocol: Republican members of Congress must:

  1. Never publicly contradict POTUS on any matter

  2. Defend all presidential actions, regardless of previous stated positions

  3. Attack anyone who questions POTUS with equal or greater vigor than POTUS himself

  4. Preface all statements with loyalty declarations

  5. Understand that committee chairmanships, campaign funding, and endorsements flow from loyalty scores

Case Study - Senator Lindsey Graham:

2016 Primary: “I think he’s a kook. I think he’s crazy. I think he’s unfit for office.”

2017-2025: “President Trump is the strongest leader we’ve had in generations. Everything he does is strategic and brilliant.”

Protocol Assessment: Senator Graham demonstrates that early resistance can be overcome through sustained, enthusiastic submission. The key is never referencing the previous position and maintaining complete consistency in the new loyalty position.

Case Study - Senator Ted Cruz:

2016: After Trump insulted his wife and suggested his father was involved in JFK assassination: “This man is a pathological liar.”

2017-2025: Became one of Trump’s most vocal defenders, attended multiple Mar-a-Lago dinners, sought Trump’s endorsement for reelection.

Protocol Assessment: Cruz demonstrates that even the most personal attacks can be overcome through proper submission. The lesson: pride is expensive, compliance is profitable.

Category E: The State-Level Replication

Required Protocol: Governors and state officials in Republican states must:

  1. Mirror federal loyalty protocols in state governments

  2. Require state agency heads to pledge allegiance to Trump’s agenda

  3. Punish local officials who question federal policies

  4. Ensure state resources support federal initiatives

  5. Compete for presidential favor through increasingly dramatic loyalty displays

Example - Governor Ron DeSantis (Florida):

2024 Primary Challenge: Attempted to position himself as Trump alternative, suggested it was “time to move on” from 2020 election claims.

Post-Primary: Complete submission, endorsed Trump, eliminated all criticism from public statements, ensured Florida state apparatus supported Trump agenda.

Protocol Assessment: DeSantis learned that challenging the king before he abdicates results in permanent diminishment. His presidential ambitions now depend entirely on Trump’s blessing. The lesson: wait for the throne to empty naturally, never attempt to accelerate the process.

PART II: FIFA’S SPECIAL QUALIFICATIONS

Why a Corruption Icon Makes the Perfect Tribute Bearer

The FIFA Corruption Heritage

For those unfamiliar with FIFA’s distinguished history in institutional corruption:

The 2015 Scandal:

  • U.S. Department of Justice indicted 14 FIFA officials

  • Charges: Racketeering, wire fraud, money laundering

  • Scheme involved $150+ million in bribes

  • Bribery related to World Cup bids, marketing rights

  • FIFA President Sepp Blatter resigned (later banned from football)

The Pattern:

  • Decades of bribery in tournament site selection

  • World Cup votes for sale to highest bidder

  • Qatar 2022: Alleged bribes, human rights concerns, thousands of migrant worker deaths

  • Russia 2018: Similar concerns

The Qualification:

  • Organization synonymous with corruption awards “peace prize”

  • Organization that banned players for political messages creates political award

  • Organization currently under reform mandates invents presidential tribute

The Perfect Irony: FIFA, an organization that professionalized corruption in international sport, now judges who deserves a peace prize.

The Infantino Factor

Gianni Infantino’s Background:

  • Elected FIFA president in 2016 as “reform candidate”

  • Promised to clean up organization after corruption scandals

  • Instead: Expanded presidential powers, reduced transparency

  • Moved to Qatar (tax advantages)

  • Increased FIFA revenues while decreasing accountability

The Trump Relationship:

  • Calculated alignment with Trump administration

  • Multiple White House visits

  • Presence at Trump inauguration (2025)

  • Attendance at peace ceremonies

  • Strategic positioning for 2026 World Cup

The Calculation: Infantino recognized that 2026 World Cup success in U.S. depends on Trump favor. What’s a made-up peace prize compared to FIFA’s financial interests?

The Broader Lesson

Why FIFA’s Corruption History Matters:

The Trump system attracts those already comfortable with transactional, corrupt relationships. FIFA didn’t have to adjust its institutional culture to participate in Trump’s feudal system—it already operated on the same principles:

  1. Power through personal relationships, not institutions

  2. Money flows to those who show loyalty

  3. Transparency is weakness

  4. Rules are for others

  5. Whatever serves the leader serves the organization

FIFA and Trump were made for each other. The corruption was already present; they simply recognized kindred spirits.

The Self-Coronation Redux: When Trump placed that gold medal around his own neck on December 5, 2025, he wasn’t just accepting a tribute. He was demonstrating the fundamental principle of his second term: When the world won’t crown you properly, crown yourself. When institutions won’t validate you, create new institutions. When reality disappoints, manufacture a better reality.

Gianni Infantino understood this perfectly. That’s why he invented the FIFA Peace Prize in the first place.

The tribute system documented above represents the minimum requirements for maintaining access to power in the Trump administration. These are not suggestions. They are prerequisites.

Bow deeply. Bow publicly. Bow often. Or prepare to lose your head.

Metaphorically speaking, of course.
