The Propaganda Pivot

How Kennedy Weaponized Federal Health Agencies to Spread Vaccine Misinformation

December 28, 2025

Part of the RFK Files series documenting the systematic dismantling of America’s public health infrastructure

December 28, 2025

Bottom Line Up Front

The threat Robert F. Kennedy Jr. poses has evolved beyond the institutional destruction I documented in earlier essays. Kennedy has now transformed federal health agencies into active propaganda organs spreading vaccine misinformation with government authority. When the CDC website promotes debunked vaccine-autism correlations, we face a fundamentally different crisis than budget cuts or personnel changes.

This is weaponized disinformation deployed through America’s most trusted public health institutions—creating damage that may prove irreversible even after Kennedy leaves office.

The Corruption of Federal Authority

When “The CDC Says” Becomes Disinformation

My earlier analyses documented how Kennedy dismantled public health infrastructure—terminating mRNA research, forcing out scientific leadership, undermining pandemic preparedness. That destruction was devastating but conceptually reversible: funding restored, experts rehired, programs rebuilt.

What Kennedy has done since represents something far more insidious.

As documented in a December 28 New York Times analysis by Dr. Benjamin Mazer, a physician who spent years tracking anti-vaccine groups, Kennedy has corrupted the CDC’s own messaging. The agency’s website, once declaring vaccines don’t cause autism, now reads:

“The rise in autism prevalence since the 1980s correlates with the rise in the number of vaccines given to infants.”

This statement appears nearly verbatim on Children’s Health Defense’s website—the anti-vaccine organization Kennedy formerly led. Studies have repeatedly proven it false. But it sounds “faintly data-driven,” as Dr. Mazer notes, while exploiting concerns about overmedicalization.

The Strategic Brilliance of This Corruption

Parents refusing vaccines now cite “the CDC” as authority. School officials see CDC content supporting parental concerns. Pediatricians encounter patients armed with government websites backing vaccine hesitancy.

Kennedy hasn’t just silenced scientific voices—he’s hijacked their institutional credibility to amplify anti-vaccine messaging. As Dr. Mazer writes: “The government itself has become a propaganda organ for the movement.”

This represents information warfare against the American public, executed by agencies charged with protecting public health.

The Professionalization of Anti-Science

From Fringe Conferences to Federal Policy

Dr. Mazer reveals how anti-vaccine groups spent decades perfecting persuasive techniques at “dinky conferences and obscure media outlets.” They learned to “stoke suspicion of authority with masterful precision” and apply “a veneer of scientific legitimacy” to debunked theories.

What changed under Kennedy isn’t the sophistication—it’s the platform.

Those propaganda techniques, once confined to fringe websites, now emanate from CDC.gov and HHS press releases. Rhetorical tactics medical professionals dismissed as easily debunked now carry federal authority’s imprimatur.

The Deliberate Ambiguity Strategy

Kennedy and appointees have mastered sowing doubt without falsifiable claims. Acting CDC Director Jim O’Neill’s December response to measles outbreaks: rather than endorse universal vaccination, only a “vague recommendation for parents to ‘consult with their health care providers about vaccination options.’”

This ambiguity:

  • Provides plausible deniability (”We never told people not to vaccinate”)

  • Undermines physician authority by framing vaccination as “personal choice”

  • Exploits asymmetry where doctors must prove safety while skeptics merely raise questions

  • Creates permission structure for vaccine refusal with federal backing

As Kennedy wrote in March: “The decision to vaccinate is a personal one”—branding opposition to proven interventions as individual liberty advocacy.

The September 4 Reckoning: Even Allies Sound Alarms

When the Hearing Room Became an Intervention

Kennedy’s Senate Finance Committee appearance on September 4, 2025, marked a critical inflection point. Two hundred days into his tenure, facing bipartisan questioning about CDC purge and vaccine changes, Kennedy demonstrated how presidential enablement has emboldened his anti-science agenda.

The hearing was, NBC News reported, “unusually confrontational” and “the harshest congressional oversight of any Cabinet official since the start of Donald Trump’s second term.”

The Trustworthiness Lie

Kennedy claimed he fired CDC Director Susan Monarez because when he asked “Are you a trustworthy person?” she answered “No.”

Senator Elizabeth Warren pressed him. Kennedy doubled down. Senator Bernie Sanders, incredulous, asked Kennedy to confirm the “former head of CDC” declared herself untrustworthy. Kennedy clarified: “She didn’t say, ‘No, I’m not a trustworthy person.’ She said, ‘No.’”

Monarez’s attorneys responded immediately: Monarez “stands by” her account and “would repeat it all under oath.”

This transparent fabrication reveals Kennedy’s contempt for Senate oversight and basic factual accuracy—plus brazenness from knowing Trump supports him regardless.

Republican Physicians Sound Warnings

Most significantly, even GOP physician-senators expressed alarm:

Senator John Barrasso (R-Wyoming), a physician: “grown increasingly worried about Robert F. Kennedy Jr.’s stance on vaccines and the potential erosion of public trust.”

Senator Bill Cassidy (R-Louisiana), physician who voted to confirm Kennedy after securing commitments: “I need to know why his words in the confirmation hearing aren’t matching up with some of the deeds.”

Senator Thom Tillis (R-North Carolina) confronted Kennedy about making vaccines harder to access: “There seem to be several reports that would seem to refute” Kennedy’s denials.

When Trump’s own physician-senators express alarm, it underscores how far Kennedy has strayed from scientific consensus.

The Medical Community Confrontation

Bernie Sanders challenged Kennedy’s claims that medical organizations are “corrupted by the pharmaceutical industry”:

“You’re telling the American people that the American Medical Association, representing hundreds of thousands of people, have been coopted and that they should not trust their doctors? And the American Academy of Pediatrics?”

Kennedy: “The American Heart Association has been corrupted by the pharma industry.”

This wholesale dismissal of medical expertise—AMA, American Academy of Pediatrics, American Heart Association—reveals Kennedy’s strategy: when the entire medical establishment opposes you, declare the establishment corrupt.

The Retreat From Oversight: When Accountability Becomes Optional

Kennedy’s Refusal to Testify After September Bruising

The September hearing proved so damaging that Kennedy has refused to honor his commitment to regular congressional testimony—revealing consciousness of guilt and systematic evasion.

The Broken Promise

During confirmation, Kennedy promised quarterly testimony before the Senate Health Committee—helping secure crucial votes from skeptical senators like Cassidy. Kennedy last appeared before that committee in May 2025.

Since the contentious September Finance Committee hearing, Kennedy has refused multiple requests. Bernie Sanders criticized this refusal as undermining “congressional oversight” when Kennedy’s actions—mass scientific firings, dramatic vaccine policy changes—demand explanation.

The Subpoena That Republicans Blocked

The evasion reached a critical point mid-December when Representative Frank Pallone (D-New Jersey) attempted to subpoena Kennedy about vaccine policy changes. House Republicans voted down the attempt, providing Kennedy cover to continue avoiding accountability.

Pallone had made clear why testimony was essential: “The bottom line is here: We have no transparency. You feel no responsibility to Congress whatsoever, and you just continue this ideology that’s anti-science, anti-vaccine.”

The Pattern of Evasion

Kennedy’s retreat follows predictable pattern among officials whose actions cannot withstand scrutiny:

  1. May 2025: Last Senate Health Committee appearance

  2. September 4, 2025: Bruising Finance Committee hearing where even GOP senators challenged credibility

  3. September-December 2025: Multiple refusals despite quarterly promise

  4. Mid-December 2025: House Republicans block Democratic subpoena

This systematic evasion violates confirmation commitments and basic congressional oversight principles.

The Constitutional Implications

Cabinet officials serve at presidential pleasure but remain accountable to Congress through oversight and appropriations powers. When officials refuse testimony despite confirmation commitments, they create constitutional crisis where executive power operates without legislative checks.

Cassidy, who confirmed Kennedy based on explicit vaccine policy commitments, now faces consequences of trusting those promises. His committee has HHS oversight responsibility, yet the secretary he confirmed refuses to appear and explain systematic violations.

This pattern—secure confirmation through promises, violate systematically, refuse testimony about violations—represents exactly the bad faith that confirmation hearings prevent.

The Information Warfare Dimension

When Your Own Government Becomes the Threat

National security professionals understand information warfare doesn’t always come from foreign adversaries. When federal health agencies spread misinformation, they create vulnerabilities enemies need not exploit—we’re doing damage ourselves.

The Irreversibility Problem

Even if Kennedy were removed tomorrow, institutional credibility damage cannot be quickly undone. Parents who learned from CDC.gov that vaccines correlate with autism won’t suddenly trust the same website when it reverses course. School boards won’t easily reverse vaccine-skeptical policies citing federal guidance. Physicians face impossible tasks rebuilding trust after government-backed misinformation.

This is why Kennedy’s corruption of federal messaging represents qualitatively different threat than budget cuts or personnel decisions. You can restore funding and rehire scientists. You cannot quickly restore faith in institutions that deliberately lied.

The Adversary Advantage

China, Russia, and strategic competitors monitor U.S. public health capabilities as national resilience indicators. When America’s own health secretary undermines vaccination programs and spreads anti-science messaging, adversaries gain multiple advantages:

  • Biological vulnerability: Reduced vaccination rates create opportunities for natural and engineered pathogens

  • Technological gaps: mRNA research cuts cede biotechnology leadership

  • Institutional weakness: Agency chaos suggests systemic dysfunction

  • Propaganda value: American self-sabotage validates authoritarian critiques

Kennedy’s tenure provides adversaries exactly what decades of their propaganda operations struggled to achieve: systematic undermining of American public health capacity with full presidential support.

Why Medical Organizations Must Escalate

The Inadequacy of Just Stating Facts

Dr. Mazer, who spent years debunking anti-vaccine misinformation, makes sobering observation: “Vaccines may be safe and effective, but just saying so is no longer enough.”

The medical community must adapt to landscapes where federal agencies actively undermine vaccination programs:

Aggressive Counter-Messaging: Medical organizations must “ramp up their efforts to disseminate persuasive rebuttals to anti-vaccine talking points wherever people get their health information”—meeting patients where they are rather than assuming institutional authority carries weight.

Emotional Connection Over Data: “It is important for medical professionals to express how much they care, not just how much they know.” The anti-vaccine community “serves as a home for people who feel neglected or dismissed by conventional medicine.”

Firsthand Experience: “Many doctors have witnessed the harms of vaccine-preventable diseases. Relaying those firsthand experiences will be more compelling than simply dishing out data.”

Congressional Authority in the Propaganda Era

Why Kennedy’s Misinformation Campaign Demands Legislative Response

Earlier essays documented why Congress possesses clear constitutional authority to remove Kennedy based on confirmation violations and pandemic preparedness destruction. The federal health messaging corruption strengthens that case:

False Testimony Implications

Kennedy assured senators during confirmation: “If confirmed, I will do nothing as HHS secretary that makes it difficult or discourages people from taking vaccines.” Instructing CDC to promote vaccine-autism correlations and refusing to endorse vaccination during measles outbreaks directly violates this commitment.

His refusal to testify since September—despite promising quarterly appearances—compounds this breach. Senators who confirmed Kennedy based on assurances now face consequences of trusting systematic lies.

Appropriations Authority

Congress funds CDC expecting accurate, evidence-based health guidance. When appropriated funds support disinformation campaigns, Congress faces constitutional duty and moral imperative to intervene.

Oversight Responsibility

Senate confirmation presumes nominees operating in good faith within expertise. Kennedy’s transformation of health agencies into propaganda organs represents fundamental breach of confirmation compact.

His subsequent refusal to explain these actions before committees that confirmed him demonstrates contempt for congressional oversight itself.

Conclusion: The Urgency of Now

Robert F. Kennedy Jr.’s first year as Health Secretary has validated warnings about dangers of appointing conspiracy theorists to public trust positions. But the threat has evolved beyond even harshest critics’ predictions.

Kennedy hasn’t just dismantled public health infrastructure—he’s weaponized it against the American people. The same federal agencies that once served as bulwarks against disease and misinformation now actively spread anti-vaccine propaganda with full government authority.

The damage compounds daily:

  • More parents refuse vaccines citing corrupted CDC guidance

  • More children contract preventable diseases as immunization rates fall

  • More trust erodes in institutions requiring decades to rebuild

  • More adversaries exploit self-inflicted American vulnerabilities

Kennedy’s retreat from oversight reveals consciousness of guilt:

  • Promised quarterly testimony to secure confirmation

  • Last appeared before Senate Health Committee in May

  • Refused multiple requests after bruising September hearing

  • Republicans blocked Democratic subpoena attempt in December

Congress faces stark choice:

Act immediately to remove Kennedy and begin the long process of restoring institutional credibility, or accept responsibility for every child contracting preventable diseases while government itself undermines vaccination programs.

The propaganda pivot isn’t just another policy failure. It represents weaponization of federal authority against scientific truth and public health—with American children’s lives hanging in balance.

As Dr. Mazer concludes: “We’re only one year in, and how physicians and public health experts respond will shape what happens next.”

Congress must ensure there isn’t a second year. Kennedy’s refusal to face congressional oversight after systematically violating confirmation commitments proves he cannot defend his actions. Officials who cannot defend policies before representatives of the American people have no business implementing those policies.

The time for removal is now—before more children die, before more institutional damage becomes irreversible, and before Kennedy’s corruption of federal health messaging destroys last vestiges of public trust in agencies that took generations to build.
