The Heritage Foundation's Christian Nationalist Vision

Project 2025, formally titled "Mandate for Leadership: The Conservative Promise 2025," represents the most comprehensive plan for Christian nationalist governance in American history. The Heritage Foundation's blueprint explicitly calls for establishing a government "imbued with biblical principles" run by a president with "sweeping executive powers."

The plan's scope is breathtaking:

  • 920+ pages of detailed policy recommendations

  • A recruitment database of ideologically vetted candidates based on political and religious loyalty

  • The Presidential Administration Academy to indoctrinate would-be employees

  • The "180-Day Playbook" assigning transition teams to implement agency-by-agency changes immediately

Heritage Foundation president Kevin Roberts made the stakes clear: "We are in the process of the second American Revolution, which will remain bloodless if the left allows it to be." This isn't hyperbole—it's a direct threat wrapped in revolutionary language.

Critics correctly identified Project 2025 as a blueprint for authoritarian Christian nationalism that would fundamentally transform American governance from democratic institutions serving all citizens to religious authoritarianism serving only conservative Christians.

The Campaign of Denial

Clockwise from top left: Kevin Roberts, Rick Dearborn and Russell Voight.

Throughout the 2024 campaign, Trump repeatedly and vehemently denied any connection to Project 2025. His denials weren't casual dismissals—they were emphatic rejections designed to distance himself from the plan's most controversial elements.

"I know nothing about Project 2025," Trump claimed at multiple campaign events. "I have no idea who is behind it." He called some recommendations "absolutely ridiculous and abysmal" and insisted he disagreed with their approach.

These denials were strategic lies designed to avoid electoral consequences while preserving the ability to implement the agenda after winning. Trump understood that Project 2025's explicit Christian nationalism would alienate moderate voters necessary for victory.

The campaign denials represent a massive violation of the Ninth Commandment against bearing false witness. Trump systematically deceived voters about his intentions while planning to implement the very agenda he publicly rejected.

The Implementation: Promises Kept, Voters Betrayed

Since taking office, Trump has implemented dozens of Project 2025 recommendations through executive orders, agency directives, and personnel decisions. The systematic implementation reveals that the campaign denials were calculated deception.

Mr. Trump and Russell T. Vought in 2019. Mr. Vought led a section of Project 2025 that dealt with executive orders. In late November, as president-elect, Mr. Trump chose him to lead the Office of Management and Budget.Credit...Evan Vucci/Associated Press

The most damning evidence came within weeks of inauguration: Trump's appointment of Russell Vought to lead the Office of Management and Budget. Vought wasn't just connected to Project 2025—he was a key architect, having "led a section of Project 2025 that dealt with executive orders" according to the New York Times. He also served as policy director for the 2024 Republican National Convention.

This appointment alone exposes the lie at the heart of Trump's campaign denials. He claimed to know nothing about Project 2025 or the people behind it, then immediately appointed one of its primary authors to a crucial Cabinet position. The systematic deception couldn't be more obvious.

Heritage Foundation's Victory Lap

The Heritage Foundation's response to Trump's victory revealed the coordinated nature of the deception. Within hours of Trump's electoral victory, Heritage Foundation president Kevin Roberts issued a statement celebrating that "the entire conservative movement stands united behind him" as he prepares to implement their agenda.

Kevin Roberts, the president of the Heritage Foundation, has said that he views Heritage’s role as “institutionalizing Trumpism.”Credit...Benjamin Norman for The New York Times

Roberts' immediate victory statement directly contradicted Trump's campaign claims of ignorance about Project 2025. The Heritage Foundation clearly expected Trump to implement their blueprint, because they had been coordinating with him throughout the campaign despite his public denials.

Dismantling the Administrative State

Project 2025's central goal is "dismantling the administrative state" by removing secular expertise from government and replacing it with ideological loyalty. The administration has pursued this goal through:

Schedule F Reinstatement: Trump immediately reinstated Schedule F employment classification, reclassifying tens of thousands of civil servants as political appointees who can be fired for ideological reasons rather than performance.

Agency Purges: Across government, career professionals have been systematically removed and replaced with ideologically vetted candidates whose primary qualification is loyalty to Christian nationalist principles rather than professional expertise.

Expertise Elimination: Scientific, medical, educational, and policy expertise has been subordinated to religious ideology, transforming evidence-based governance into faith-based authoritarianism.

Department of Education: Targeted for Elimination

Project 2025 explicitly calls for abolishing the Department of Education to remove secular oversight from schooling. The administration has pursued this goal through:

Funding Cuts: Systematic reduction of federal education funding while expanding voucher programs that redirect public money to private religious schools.

Curriculum Control: Implementation of anti-"woke" policies that eliminate terms like "gender equality" and "reproductive rights" from federal education materials.

Religious School Preference: Voucher programs that allow religious schools to discriminate based on sexual orientation, gender identity, or religious belief while receiving taxpayer funding.

Health and Human Services: Biblical Principles Over Medical Science

Project 2025 calls for restructuring HHS to prioritize "biblical principles" over medical expertise. The administration has implemented this through:

Science Rejection: Mass firing of health professionals who base recommendations on medical evidence rather than religious doctrine.

Alternative Medicine Promotion: Elevation of unproven alternative health approaches over established medical interventions.

LGBTQ+ Healthcare Elimination: Systematic removal of gender-affirming care and other healthcare services for LGBTQ+ individuals.

Department of Justice: Weaponizing Law Enforcement

Project 2025 envisions transforming DOJ into an enforcement arm for Christian nationalist policies. The administration has implemented this through:

Anti-Christian Bias Task Force: Creation of DOJ units specifically designed to investigate prosecutions of conservative Christians while ignoring violence against non-Christians.

Religious Exemption Enforcement: Using federal law enforcement to create and defend religious exemptions from generally applicable laws for conservative Christians only.

Secular Prosecution: Targeting secular organizations and leaders while protecting religious conservatives who violate the same laws.

The Personnel Pipeline: Ideological Vetting

Project 2025's most insidious element is its comprehensive personnel operation designed to staff the government with ideologically pure candidates. The administration has implemented this through:

The Database: A comprehensive catalog of candidates vetted for political and religious loyalty rather than professional qualifications.

Loyalty Tests: Systematic application of ideological and theological litmus tests for government positions, violating constitutional prohibitions on religious tests for office.

Academy Graduates: Placement of Presidential Administration Academy graduates in key positions throughout government, ensuring ideological conformity across agencies.

This personnel operation represents the systematic establishment of religious tests for public office, directly violating Article VI of the Constitution.
